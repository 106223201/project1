Position: Data Analyst

Reference No: DA102

Reports to: Senior Data Scientist

Salary Range: AUD 80,000 – 100,000 per year

Position Description

The Data Analyst will collect, clean, and interpret data to help drive strategic decisions across the organization.

Key Responsibilities

  • Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets to identify patterns and trends.
  • Develop dashboards and reports for internal stakeholders.
  • Collaborate with software and AI teams to ensure data quality and accessibility.
  • Assist in automating data processes and maintaining databases.

Required Qualifications, Skills, and Attributes

Essential

  1. Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field.
  2. At least 2 years of experience in data analysis or a related role.
  3. Proficiency in SQL, Python, and Power BI/Tableau.
  4. Strong communication and problem-solving skills.

Preferable

  • Experience with machine learning techniques.
  • Knowledge of cloud data platforms (AWS, Azure).

Position: AI/ML Engineer

Reference No: AI657

Reports to: Head of Artificial Intelligence

Salary Range: AUD 110,000 – 130,000 per year

Position Description

The AI/ML Engineer will design and deploy intelligent systems to improve data-driven decision-making and optimize educational products.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop, train, and deploy machine learning models for predictive analytics.
  • Collaborate with data scientists to build scalable AI pipelines.
  • Monitor model performance and retrain as necessary.
  • Contribute to research and innovation projects involving deep learning and NLP.

Required Qualifications, Skills, and Attributes

Essential

  1. Bachelor’s or Master’s in Computer Science, AI, or related field.
  2. 3+ years of experience in AI/ML model development and deployment.
  3. Proficiency in Python, TensorFlow, or PyTorch.
  4. Understanding of data pipelines and APIs.

Preferable

  • Experience with cloud-based ML platforms (AWS Sagemaker, Azure ML).
  • Knowledge of reinforcement learning or LLM fine-tuning.
